PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

Blinds


Window blinds give you more control over the amount of light you allow into your space. Blinds can tilt, close fully or open to let in the right amount of light into your room.

Curtains


Curtain, in interior design, decorative fabric commonly hung to regulate the admission of light at windows and to prevent drafts from door or window openings.

Folding Door


The folding door or accordion doors will slide and close, as an accordion collapsed together. The doors will leave a wide-open space when they are opened fully.

Wallpapers


Wallpaper is a material used in interior decoration to decorate the interior walls of domestic and public buildings. It is usually sold in rolls and is applied onto a wall using wallpaper paste.

Door Lock


When you have electronic door locks, you will be able to remotely lock and unlock your doors as well as monitor whether they are locked or not. You can use a key fob, your car, a mobile application, or a key code to unlock them. These were first found on cars, they are now available in homes and apartments as well.

Sail Shades


A shade sail offers greater protection against skin cancer, as it covers large spaces. The fabric of the sail has been designed to prevent UV and UVB rays from reaching a person's body. In addition, these shades can be configured to fit in outdoor spaces that have irregular shapes.

ABOUT US

Our Story


JN4D Interior Design Services started as a distributor of window coverings, accordion and folding doors in 2016, became one of the country’s big hit suppliers of window shades and is registered to Department of Trade and Industry in 2019.
Our team is composed of young professionals and dedicated people with great knowledge and committed to excellence. Their experience built strong trust and confidence among clients in different sectors such as Houses, Restaurants, Churches and Offices.
JN4D Interior Design Services is partnered with the exclusive importer of window coverings in the country. Through this, we are capable of utilizing high quality of materials and more competitive in price.
JN4D Interior Design Services, a young company, is aiming for success in Interior Design industry. Since establishment, we continue to create, innovate and discover new style in interior decorations by incorporating the ideas of our clients. We always make sure that the trust that was given to us is served well with integrity, professionalism and commitment.
We are committed to give nothing but satisfaction to our valued customers by providing high quality and affordable products and commendable services.
